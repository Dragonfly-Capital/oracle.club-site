Tellor

About

Tellor is a decentralized oracle that uses staked PoW miners that compete to push requested data to the Ethereum network. The median of the first five miners to successfully submit the data becomes the official value and the validity of the values can be disputed for a fee. The dispute goes to a vote and if the miner is determined to be malicious, their stake is given to the disputer, otherwise the dispute fee goes to the disputed miner.

Data specifications are defined off-chain and include a range of values stemming from public API's including median prices from multiple exchanges, EOD values, and several TWAP values. Focused on creating a fully decentralized oracle, each piece is crypto-economically secured and relies on token holders rather than any central parties or reputation.
